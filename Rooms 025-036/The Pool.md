Cost: 1 [[Gems]]
An indoor Orindian pool was one of the more modern additions to the estate, the inclusion of which provided a convenient excuse for building a handful of other rooms which not only complement an indoor pool, but are often compulsory inclusions.

- Adds [[Locker Room]], [[Sauna]], and [[Pump Room]] to the Draft Pool
- Can be drained via the [[Pump Room]]
- After finding Room 46, Swim Trunks can be purchased from the [[Gift Shop]] to add two chests to The Pool. These trunks can only be opened if the pool is drained.
